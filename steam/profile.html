What is Game Database?

🎮 Your Gaming Analytics Hub

gdb.gg is a comprehensive gaming statistics platform that tracks player data, game metrics, and leaderboards across multiple gaming platforms.

👤 Steam Profile Features

  • Steam level and badge showcase
  • Recently played games tracking
  • Complete game library with playtime stats
  • Friends list with online status
  • VAC and game ban status checking

🌐 Other Platforms

We also track statistics for:

  • Fortnite - Battle Royale stats and leaderboards
  • Twitch - Streamer analytics and viewership
  • More platforms coming soon!

🔒 Privacy Note

Some profile data may be unavailable depending on the user's Steam privacy settings. We only display publicly available information.

What is Game Database?

🎮 Your Gaming Analytics Hub

gdb.gg is a comprehensive gaming statistics platform that tracks player data, game metrics, and leaderboards across multiple gaming platforms.

🔵 Steam Tracker Features

  • Real-time player counts for thousands of games
  • Featured, trending, and new release game discovery
  • Player profile lookup and statistics
  • Game details with screenshots, news, and pricing
  • Top sellers and special deals tracking

🌐 Other Platforms

We also track statistics for:

  • Fortnite - Battle Royale stats and leaderboards
  • Twitch - Streamer analytics and viewership
  • More platforms coming soon!

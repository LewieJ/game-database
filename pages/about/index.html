About

GDB.GG is home to fan-made game databases — fast, clean, and genuinely useful for the games we love.

What we build

We build dedicated reference hubs for individual games: Marathon DB is live now, with GTA 6 Central in development. Each site covers that game's items, loadouts, and cosmetics in depth.

Why it exists

  • Speed first: quick loads and lightweight pages.
  • Useful data: focused on info you actually look for.
  • Community feedback: fixes and improvements based on real usage.

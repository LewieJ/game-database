About

GDB.GG is a community-driven gaming stats and reference platform. We’re focused on making pages fast, clean, and genuinely useful — with no clutter.

What we’re building

We’re starting with Marathon — weapons, runners, cosmetics, and competitive tools — and expanding into more games and platforms over time.

Why it exists

  • Speed first: quick loads and lightweight pages.
  • Useful data: focused on info you actually look for.
  • Community feedback: fixes and improvements based on real usage.

Competitive Score (CS)

Fair, cross-event ranking system

📊 Main Formula

Window Score = (Placement Points + Performance Points) × Event Multiplier

Your total CS is the sum of all your window scores across every competitive event.

🎯 Placement Points (Percentile-Based)

Fair across all event sizes - rewards top percentiles equally whether 50 or 10,000 players compete:

Percentile Points Example (10,000 players)
Top 0.1% 100 Ranks 1-10
Top 0.5% 90 Ranks 1-50
Top 1% 75 Ranks 1-100
Top 3% 60 Ranks 1-300
Top 5% 50 Ranks 1-500
Top 10% 40 Ranks 1-1,000
Top 25% 25 Ranks 1-2,500
Top 50% 10 Ranks 1-5,000

⚡ Performance Points

Performance = Eliminations + (Wins × 15) + Consistency Bonus

Eliminations

1 point per elimination earned during the event

Win Bonus

15 points for each Victory Royale

Consistency

+5 points if you competed in 5+ matches

🏆 Event Multipliers

Different tournaments have different prestige levels:

Event Type Multiplier Examples
FNCS Championships 4.0× Finals, Grand Finals
FNCS Qualifiers 2.0× Major, Invitational
Cash Cups 1.5× Trio Cash Cup, Elite Cup
Standard Cups 1.0× Zero Build, Reload Cups
Mobile Series 0.5× Mobile-only tournaments

👥 Team Scoring

All teammates receive identical scores to prevent toxic behavior and fairly reward team contribution. Whether you're in duos, trios, or squads, your team's performance is your performance.

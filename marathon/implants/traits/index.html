Implant Traits

Every randomised implant trait in Marathon — perks that can roll on implant drops

About Implant Traits

Traits are randomised perks that can roll on implant drops in Marathon. Each time you find an implant, it may come with one or more traits from the pool below. Think of them like weapon rolls in Destiny 2 — the same implant can drop with different trait combinations.

This page lists every known trait in the game. Use the search box to quickly find a specific trait by name or description.

About Marathon Player Profiles

What Are Marathon Player Profiles?

Marathon player profiles on MarathonDB will allow you to search for any runner by their Bungie Name and view comprehensive stats, match history, and ranked progression. Similar to stat trackers for other competitive shooters, MarathonDB aims to be the go-to destination for Marathon player data once Bungie opens API access.

What Stats Will Be Tracked?

We plan to track extraction success rates, kill/death ratios, total loot value extracted, favorite loadouts, time played per runner class, weapon usage statistics, and more. Profiles will show both lifetime stats and season-by-season breakdowns.

Ranked Tracking

Marathon's ranked mode features a unique zone-based competitive system. Player profiles will display your current rank — from Bronze through Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Pinnacle, and the elusive Master tier — along with subdivision progress (I, II, III) and historical rank data across seasons.

Leaderboards Integration

Player profiles will be directly linked to our leaderboards, allowing you to see where any player ranks globally and regionally across ranked and general playlists.

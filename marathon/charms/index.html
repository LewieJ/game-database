About Marathon Weapon Charms

Marathon Weapon Charms

Weapon charms in Marathon are small cosmetic accessories that hang from your equipped weapon. They're visible during gameplay in first person and to other players who pick up your gun. Charms don't affect stats or performance at all. They come in different rarity tiers and can be obtained through the in-game store, with more expected from battle passes, events, and potentially in-game challenges. We track every charm in the game so you can browse by rarity and see where each one comes from.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do weapon charms affect gameplay?

No. Weapon charms are purely cosmetic. They hang from your weapon as a visual accessory but don't change damage, handling, or any other stat.

How do you get weapon charms in Marathon?

Charms are available through the in-game store and are expected to appear in battle passes and seasonal content. Some may also be earnable through events or in-game challenges.

How does MarathonDB get its charm data?

We find every charm in-game, screenshot it, and enter the data by hand. We don't rely on a Bungie API, everything you see here was manually cataloged.

Is this database kept up to date?

Yes. We track all Marathon cosmetics across every platform and update when new content drops. The goal is to maintain the most accurate database possible.

Can I submit corrections or missing charms?

Absolutely. If you spot anything wrong or missing, message us on Discord and we'll get it fixed.

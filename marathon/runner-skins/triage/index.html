About Marathon Triage Skins

Marathon Triage Skins

The Triage is Marathon's damage mitigation specialist — a runner designed for players who want to sustain their team, heal through fights, and keep the squad alive during extraction runs. Triage skins let you customise the look of your Triage with unique shells, colour variants, themed outfits, and limited-edition cosmetics. Every skin on this page is purely cosmetic and has zero impact on gameplay stats, abilities, or hitboxes.

Triage skins range from default appearances to Exotic-rarity shells available through the in-game store, seasonal passes, pre-order bonuses, faction rewards, and limited-time event bundles. Frequently Asked Questions

How many Triage skins are in Marathon?

Marathon features a growing collection of Triage skins including default shells, legendary outfits, and exotic cosmetics. New Triage skins are added with seasonal updates, limited-time events, and store rotations. Check the grid above for the current full count.

Are Triage skins purely cosmetic?

Yes. All Triage skins in Marathon are 100% cosmetic. They change the visual appearance of the Triage runner but do not affect hitboxes, abilities, healing output, or any gameplay statistics.

How do I get Triage skins in Marathon?

Triage skins can be obtained through multiple sources: the in-game store, seasonal battle pass rewards, pre-order and edition bonuses, limited-time event bundles, and faction reputation rewards. Some rare skins are exclusive to specific events or promotions.

What is the Triage class in Marathon?

The Triage is Marathon's damage mitigation runner class. Triages specialise in healing, shielding, and damage reduction, making them ideal for players who prefer a supportive, team-oriented playstyle in Marathon's extraction-based PvP.

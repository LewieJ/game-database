Complete Bungie Marathon weapon guide with stats, mods, and tier rankings

The complete database of all 34 weapons in Marathon, Bungie's extraction shooter. Whether you need the best Marathon assault rifle, a high-DPS shotgun, a melee weapon, or a long-range sniper pick, the data is here.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many weapons are in Marathon?

There are 34 weapons across 9 categories: Assault Rifles (5), Machine Guns (4), Melee (1), Pistols (3), Precision Rifles (7), Railguns (4), Shotguns (3), Sniper Rifles (3), and Submachine Guns (4).

Can you customise weapons with mods in Marathon?

Yes. Each weapon has a set of compatible mods that can change how it performs. The number of available mods varies per weapon and is listed on every weapon page in this database.

What stats does this database track for each weapon?

Every weapon page includes damage, fire rate, magazine size, reload speed, and range. You can also see compatible mods, available skins, and community ratings.

How do I compare weapons on this site?

Click the Compare button above the grid, then select up to four weapons. Their stats will be displayed side-by-side so you can see exactly how they stack up.

Is this weapon data kept up to date?

Yes. We update weapon stats, mods, and skins as new content and balance patches drop. If you spot anything wrong, let us know on Discord.

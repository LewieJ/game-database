About the Marathon Weapon Tier List

Use this interactive Marathon tier list maker to rank every weapon in Bungie's Marathon. Whether you're looking for the best Marathon weapons for PvP, trying to figure out the current Marathon weapon meta, or just want to compare your gun rankings with friends — this tool makes it easy. Drag and drop all 30 weapons into S, A, B, C, D, or F tier.

Use the category filters to focus on a single weapon class — rank the best Marathon assault rifles, machine guns, pistols, precision rifles, railguns, shotguns, sniper rifles, submachine guns separately, or rank them all together.

Once you've built your Marathon weapon tier list, hit Share Tier List to copy a shareable link. Anyone who opens your link will see your exact weapon rankings — perfect for sharing your Marathon tierlist on Discord, Reddit, or social media.

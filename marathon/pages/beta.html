What You'll Play During the Server Slam

The Server Slam is a slice of Marathon's gameplay. Enough to get comfortable, not enough to see everything.

You'll be able to:

  • Infil into two zones:
    • Perimeter — an edge-site expansion on the colony's outskirts
    • Dire Marsh — the colony's agricultural research hub
  • Take on early contracts for five factions: CyberAcme, NuCaloric, Traxus, MIDA, and Arachne
  • Progress through the early faction levels and progression trees
  • Try out five of the six Runner shells available at launch, plus the scavenger experience Rook
  • Play as a crew, solo Runner, form uneasy alliances with proximity chat, and more

Play the Slam, Earn Rewards for Launch

Everyone who completes the introductory mission will unlock a unique emblem and player background that marks you as part of the first wave of Runners.

Time spent during the Slam will bank loot rewards you'll receive at launch, based on how far you progress:

First Mission

Standard Arrival Cache

  • Standard implants (6x)
  • Standard Runner shell cores (4x)
  • Standard weapon chip mods (6x)
  • Weapons: Overrun and Hardline
Runner Level 10

Enhanced Arrival Cache

  • Enhanced implants (6x)
  • Enhanced Runner shell cores (2x for each shell, 12x total)
  • Enhanced weapon chip mods (4x)
  • Weapons: Enhanced Magnum and Enhanced Hardline
Runner Level 30

Deluxe Arrival Cache

  • Deluxe and Enhanced implants (3x each, 6x total)
  • Deluxe and Enhanced Runner shell cores (1x each for each shell, 12x total)
  • Deluxe and Enhanced weapon chip mods (2x each, 4x total)
  • Weapons: Deluxe Magnum and Enhanced Volley Rifle
  • Backpack: Deluxe Base Backpack

The emblem, player banner, and loot rewards will be waiting for you when Marathon launches on March 5.

Launch & Season 1

Frequently Asked Questions

When does the Marathon Server Slam start?

The Server Slam runs from February 26 at 10 AM PT to March 2 at 10 AM PT. Full launch follows on March 5, 2026.

Do I need to pre-order Marathon to play the Server Slam?

No. The Server Slam is free and open to everyone — no purchase or pre-order required.

What platforms is the Server Slam on?

Steam (PC), PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S — with cross-play and cross-save enabled.

Will my Server Slam progress carry over to launch?

Character progress does not carry over, but time spent during the Slam banks loot rewards you'll receive at launch based on your Runner Level. You'll also earn a unique emblem and player background for completing the introductory mission.

How long does the Server Slam last?

The Server Slam runs for approximately 4 days: February 26 at 10 AM PT to March 2 at 10 AM PT.

What content is available in the Server Slam?

Two zones (Perimeter and Dire Marsh), early contracts for five factions, five of six Runner shells plus Rook, early faction progression, and crew or solo play with proximity chat. It's a slice of the full experience.

What rewards can I earn in the Marathon Server Slam?

Everyone who completes the intro mission earns a unique emblem and player background. Reaching Runner Level 10 unlocks the Enhanced Arrival Cache (green-tier gear), and reaching Runner Level 30 unlocks the Deluxe Arrival Cache (blue-tier gear) including the Deluxe Magnum, Enhanced Volley Rifle, and Deluxe Base Backpack. All rewards carry over to launch day.

What Runner shells can I play in the Server Slam?

You can try five of the six Runner shells that will be available at launch, plus the scavenger experience Rook. The sixth shell, Thief, will be available at full launch on March 5.

Does the Marathon Server Slam support cross-play?

Yes. The Server Slam supports full cross-play and cross-save across Steam (PC), PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. Play with friends on any platform.

Can I preload the Marathon Server Slam?

No. Bungie has confirmed there is no preloading for the Server Slam. Downloads will go live across Steam, PlayStation Store, and Xbox Store at February 26, 10 AM PT.

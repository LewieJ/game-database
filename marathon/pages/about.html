What is this site?

gdb.gg hosts a community-driven Marathon database for Bungie's extraction shooter, Marathon. You'll find weapons, runners, items, mods, cosmetics, and more — organized for quick lookup and planning.

Our goal is to help runners make informed decisions about loadouts, understand game mechanics, and stay up to date with content and updates.

What We Offer

Weapon Database

Complete stats, damage values, mod compatibility, and detailed breakdowns for every weapon in Marathon.

Runner Profiles

Learn about each runner's abilities, loadout options, and playstyle to find your perfect match.

Cosmetics Catalog

Browse all weapon skins, runner skins, charms, emblems, backgrounds, and more.

Latest News

Stay updated with game news, patch notes, and community updates.

Community-driven

The Marathon section on gdb.gg is built by the community, for the community. We rely on data from the game, community contributions, and feedback from runners like you to keep the database accurate and current.

Have suggestions, corrections, or want to contribute? We'd love to hear from you! Visit our Contact page to get in touch.

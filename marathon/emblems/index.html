About Marathon Player Emblems

Emblems in Marathon are cosmetic icons that display on your nameplate and show up in the kill feed. They're a quick way to stand out and don't affect gameplay. Emblems come in different rarity tiers and can be obtained through the in-game store, battle passes, ranked rewards, seasonal content, and more. Frequently Asked Questions

Where do emblems show up in Marathon?

Emblems appear on your nameplate and in the kill feed during matches. They're a way to personalize your identity that other players will see throughout a game.

How do you get emblems in Marathon?

Emblems are available through the in-game store, battle passes, ranked rewards, codex challenges, seasonal events, and some are free. Each emblem's detail page shows exactly how to obtain it.

How does MarathonDB get its emblem data?

We find every emblem in-game, screenshot it, and enter the data by hand. We don't rely on a Bungie API, everything you see here was manually cataloged.

Can I rate emblems on MarathonDB?

Yes! Click any emblem to open its detail view, then use the emoji reactions to rate it. Your vote helps other players see which emblems the community loves most.

Can I submit corrections or missing emblems?

Absolutely. If you spot anything wrong or missing, message us on Discord and we'll get it fixed.

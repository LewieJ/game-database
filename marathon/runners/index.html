About this page

The complete database of all 7 playable runners in Marathon, Bungie's extraction shooter. Runners are the playable characters you choose before each match — each one brings a distinct role, a unique passive ability, and a curated pool of Cores that shape how you play. Picking the right runner for your team composition is one of the most important decisions you'll make.

The current roster spans every playstyle: Assassin and Vandal are built for aggressive flankers, Destroyer anchors frontline combat, Recon handles long-range intel, Thief rewards covert looting runs, Triage is the squad medic, and Rook is the opportunist wildcard. Every runner page includes their full ability breakdown, compatible cores, available cosmetic skins, and community ratings.

Click any runner card to dive into their detail page. Want to build out a full loadout? Jump into the Runner Loadout Builder or check the Marathon runner tier list to see how the community rates each character.

Marathon Receives Xbox Exclusive Minecraft-Inspired Cosmetics

By Chandler Bellgrave

Xbox Gets Exclusive Minecraft-Inspired Weapon Charms

Bungie has confirmed two new weapon charms that will be exclusive to Xbox Series X|S players when Marathon launches on March 5, 2026. Both charms are part of the Xbox Emerald Collection and will be automatically granted to Xbox players at launch.

The Two Exclusive Charms

Emerald Catch Weapon Charm

The first of the duo, EMERALD CATCH is a Legendary weapon charm that draws clear inspiration from Microsoft's ever-popular Minecraft franchise.

Emerald Clutch Weapon Charm

Completing the set, EMERALD CLUTCH is another Legendary weapon charm in the Xbox Emerald Collection, continuing the Minecraft-inspired aesthetic.

EMERALD CLUTCH

View Charm on MarathonDB →

Hidden References in Bungie's Reveal

The asset originally shared via Bungie's Twitter (@MarathonTheGame) contains some playful nods to the Minecraft universe. The text "CRPR" appears alongside the Emerald Clutch charm — a clear reference to Minecraft's iconic Creepers — while "MNCR" sits next to the Emerald Catch charm, short for Minecraft itself.

How to Get Them

Both weapon charms will be available exclusively to Xbox Series X|S players when Marathon launches. The charms are part of the Xbox Emerald Collection and are classified as Legendary rarity. Players on Xbox will automatically receive both charms at launch — no additional purchase required.

Marathon launches on March 5, 2026 on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and Steam.

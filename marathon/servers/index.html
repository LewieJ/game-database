About Marathon Server Status

Is Marathon Down?

This page tracks live player-reported problems for Marathon, the extraction shooter from Bungie. When players experience connection issues, matchmaking failures, lag, disconnects, crashes, or login problems, they can report them here. If many players report issues at the same time, it typically indicates a server-side outage or degraded performance affecting the Marathon game servers.

Our status indicator automatically escalates from "No Issues Detected" through "Minor Issues," "Degraded," and "Major Outage" based on the volume of incoming reports over the last 2 hours. The 24-hour chart shows historical report volume so you can see when outages started and how long they lasted.

Marathon Server Outage Tracker

Marathon uses dedicated game servers hosted on Azure infrastructure across multiple regions worldwide including US East, US West, Europe, Japan, Australia, Brazil, Southeast Asia, South Africa, and the UAE. When Bungie performs scheduled maintenance or an unexpected outage occurs, this page reflects the spike in player reports in real-time.

Common Marathon server problems include: inability to connect to game servers, extended matchmaking times, rubber-banding and high latency during matches, mid-game disconnections, failed login or authentication, game crashes to desktop, and in-game store errors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Marathon down right now?

Check the status indicator at the top of this page. It aggregates live reports from Marathon players. If many players are reporting issues simultaneously, the servers are likely experiencing problems.

How do I report Marathon server issues?

Select the issue category that matches your problem (e.g. Can't Connect, Lag, Disconnects) and click Submit Report. Your report is anonymous and rate-limited to prevent abuse.

Why can't I connect to Marathon servers?

Connection issues can be caused by server outages, scheduled maintenance, your internet connection, or firewall settings. Check this page to see if other players are experiencing similar problems. If many reports appear at once, it's likely a server-side issue.

How often does the Marathon server status update?

The status page auto-refreshes every 2 minutes and shows player reports from the last 24 hours, grouped into 30-minute intervals.

What platforms does Marathon server status cover?

This page tracks player-reported issues across all platforms — PC, PlayStation, and Xbox. Since Marathon uses shared servers with cross-play, outages typically affect all platforms simultaneously.

Is Marathon down for everyone or just me?

If the report chart shows a spike in submissions, the problem is likely affecting many players. If reports are low, the issue may be on your end — try restarting Marathon, checking your internet connection, or verifying game files through your platform's launcher.

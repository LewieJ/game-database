Marathon Weapon Builder — How It Works

Build the Best Weapon Loadout

The Marathon Weapon Builder lets you plan your primary and secondary weapon setup with full mod customization. Every weapon in the game is available — from assault rifles and SMGs to sniper rifles and heavy machine guns. Select a weapon, browse compatible mods, and watch your stats update in real time.

Mods affect every weapon stat differently. A fire rate mod on an SMG plays completely different than on a marksman rifle. The live stat preview shows you exactly what changes before you commit, so you can theory-craft the perfect setup without wasting resources in-game.

Best Weapon Perks & Mods

Not sure which mods to run? Start by identifying your weapon's weakness. Low stability? Stack stability mods. Slow reload? There's a mod for that. The builder shows you every compatible mod with its exact stat effects, so you can make informed decisions instead of guessing.

Once you've found your perfect weapon setup, hit Share to generate a unique link. Your build is saved server-side and can be loaded by anyone — perfect for sharing meta loadouts with your squad or posting guides online.
